Ozone House Dashboard

These visualizations are for the 2014 A2 DataDive. The Ozone House wants to have a better understanding of how their call data has changed over the years. We have a series of research questions we asked as a part of the DataDive. Visualizations associated with a research question (see below), have a number in their title

  1. Does the call volume increase during certain times of day?
  2. Does the call volume increase during certain times of the year?
  3. What months show the highest call volume?
  4. What are the primary "presenting issues" of the caller?
  5. Does anxiety level decrease between the start of the call and end of the call?
  6. Is end anxiety level affected by the formulation of a safety plan?
  7. Are there months in which certain presenting issues are more common than in other times of the year?
  8. Are there annual trends around the prevalence of some presenting issues?
  9. What percentage of calls with Immediate Safety Concerns have Safety Plans made?
  10. Are there any trends over time regarding how Crisis Line callers learned about Ozone House?
